Dredd

In a dystopian future, the sprawling, crime-ridden Mega-City One is policed by the Judges, who act as judge, jury, and executioner. Judge Dredd, a legendary and uncompromising enforcer, teams up with rookie Judge Cassandra Anderson on a mission to take down Ma-Ma, a ruthless drug lord controlling a 200-story tower block. As the Judges ascend the tower, they face relentless, brutal opposition in a battle for survival. Filled with intense action, gritty visuals, and a dark sense of justice, Dredd delivers a thrilling, no-holds-barred experience.

Director: Pete Travis
Starring: Karl Urban, Olivia Thirlby, Lena Headey

Tron

Sam Flynn, the tech-savvy son of Kevin Flynn, is drawn into the digital world of the Grid while investigating his father's mysterious disappearance. Inside the high-tech, neon-lit realm, Sam reunites with his father and teams up with Quorra, a skilled warrior, to confront the ruthless program CLU. With breathtaking visuals, an iconic Daft Punk soundtrack, and a thrilling blend of action and innovation, Tron: Legacy explores themes of technology, legacy, and family.

Director: Joseph Kosinski
Starring: Garrett Hedlund, Jeff Bridges, Olivia Wilde

The Internship

Two middle-aged salesmen, Billy and Nick, find themselves out of jobs in a digital world that has left them behind. In a last-ditch effort to reinvent themselves, they land internships at Google, competing against tech-savvy millennials in a cutthroat environment. Full of laughs, heart, and relatable challenges, The Internship is a comedy about second chances, adaptability, and the power of teamwork.

Director: Shawn Levy
Starring: Vince Vaughn, Owen Wilson, Rose Byrne, Max Minghella
